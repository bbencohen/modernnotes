Steps to War

  • 1937: Invasion of China
  • 1940: Invasion of Indochina
  • 1941: Dutch East indies, Pearl Harbour attacks, Philippines, Singapore
    • All the attacks happened at the same time because:
      • Limiting oil supply (6-9 months left)
      • Surprise attacks, no time for US to prepare (War Crimes)
      • No way for the US to launch a counterattack
      • Japan would fail against the US in a straight war
    • Japan takes:
      • Papua New Guinea
      • All of Indonesia
      • Thailand
      • Indochina
      • Burma
      • Manchuria / Korea
      • Philippines
      • Various other small islands

Why Japan wanted the South Pacific:

By establishing naval bases on all the atolls and islands, as well as taking out the two major US naval bases (Pearl Harbour and the Philippines), the only place America could feasibly launch an attack from would be Australia - however, if they properly militarised the area, they would have a perfect defensive perimeter
